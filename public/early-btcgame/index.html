Stake Trading, what's it about?

It's a gamified staking rewards system. Staking against BTC price action is one example of how this can be used.

Another example is sports betting (stake against your team and be rewarded over the course of a season). I'm pretty sure anything you can bet on, I can setup stake trading to emulate the action.

It takes inspiration from Pool Together which is a rewards/savings scheme with no losing scenarios. The losing scenarios for stake trading wont be zero but can be configured.

I intend to mock up another system during this hackathon.
