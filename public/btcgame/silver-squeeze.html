Rules:

Stake against the price range of your choosing.

10% is paid into the prize pool.

The amount you stake is fixed for now but you can stake as many times as you wish. (but staking more resets your rewards period currently)

When ever the silver price pings on your price range you'll recieve rewards.

There is no fee for leaving the stake. (good luck, it straight out doesnt work right yet.)

The amount of rewards to be paid per ping needs thought. It's an arbitrarily low figure for now.
